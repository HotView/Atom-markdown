## range
range函数是左闭右开型函数。
```py
for i in range(1,5,1):  
    print(i) 
1
2
3
4 
print("#########")  
for i in range(5,0,-1):  
    print(i)  
5
4
3
2
1
print("#######")  
for i in range(5,-1,-1):  
    print(i)
5
4
3
2
1
0
```
